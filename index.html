Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindGlow.ai

What is MindGlow.ai?

MindGlow.ai is a personal mental wellness assistant that combines AI chat, mood tracking, meditation, and journaling to help you achieve better mental well-being. Our app adapts to your needs and grows alongside your wellness journey.

Is MindGlow.ai a replacement for therapy?

No, MindGlow.ai is not a replacement for professional mental health care. Our app is designed to complement professional care by providing daily support, mindfulness practices, and self-reflection tools. Always consult licensed mental health professionals for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

How secure is my personal data?

We take data security seriously. All your personal information and journal entries are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal data to third parties. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

What is the Plant Care feature?

The Plant Care feature allows you to nurture virtual plants that grow alongside your wellness journey. As you maintain healthy mental wellness habits and routines, your plants flourish. This gamified approach helps motivate consistent use of wellness practices.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods.

Is MindGlow.ai available on Android?

Currently, MindGlow.ai is only available for iOS devices. We're working on an Android version and will announce its release on our website and social media channels.
